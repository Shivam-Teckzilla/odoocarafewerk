About

At Carafewerk, we want the world to look forward to preparing and enjoying their daily cups of tea. A conversation about tea with ten people in India would get you nine different methods on what constitutes a good cup. The perfect cup is really very personal.

Our mission is to make your perfect cup of tea.

Our Design Beliefs

Visual engagement

cha.i takes transparency to a completely new level for a beverage making appliance. Or any appliance for that matter.

With complete visibility on each step of tea preparation, you are invited to become a part of the tea making ritual.

Simple and elegant style

Our approach has been to look for fundamental ways to address problems. An example is cha.i’s milk container. It’s design completely eliminates the use of pipes ensuring no cleaning challenge. The same milk container can also be removed from the appliance and placed in a refrigerator when not in use.

With complete visibility on each step of tea preparation, you are invited to become a part of the tea making ritual.

Empathy

For the environment. We said no to pods for tea leaves.

For you. It is now a child’s play to make that perfect cup of tea. Even I could do it.

For the service engineer. It really is effortless to access the internals.
