See how our

Cha.i brews tea

Precision controlled induction coil first boils the water to a boil. The masala is added to the sieve. Additional items like ginger and cardamom can also be added in the sieve.

The sieve is then lowered into the water to let the contents boil for another few minutes. The masala is added and the tea is brewed precisely for 2 minutes.

Milk is then added as per the recipe. A final boil ensures that all the flavor is packed in. Lo behold, the chai is ready.
