RETURN POLICY

We have a no question asked returns policy in case of returns. If you feel that the product has not been delivered to you in a desired state and/or quality, please hand it back to our delivery team. However, as per shelf-life of the products, you must return the perishable goods and food items within 2 days of receiving the same. In case, you have already paid the balance amount will be refunded to you in the same debit card / credit card / bank account that you used to make the payment in 10 working days. If it was up to us, we would do it faster but the reversal procedures by banks take that much time.

In case you cancel your order the money will be refunded back in your debit/credit card within 7-10 working days.

For COD orders, amount will be sent at your address within next 7 working days or will be added to your preferred online wallet.
